About Us:


Today, in the film industry, the cinema has become more than just a public institution with an auditorium. In addition to a beautiful picture, the main requirements of a modern cinema goer are the choice of a movie at a convenient time for him, an armchair where, stretching out his legs, he will comfortably spend an hour and a half of the movie, a cozy and stylish place where you can have a tasty meal before the start of the screening. And how many cinemas meet such high demands?
Far from it. Therefore, the cinema "Sirius" stands apart, rightfully occupying a leading position among the cinemas of the world. Sirius cinemas already meet the high requirements of new generation cinemas: modern stylish design, high technologies for the best sound and picture transmission, comfortable and ergonomic chairs, including places for people with disabilities. But, as you know, there is no limit to perfection. Sirius makes an important contribution to the world's cultural heritage by strengthening its position in the global film industry. Sirius rightfully bears the title of a reliable and responsible business partner.
Competitive advantage - unique style and investment in quality
